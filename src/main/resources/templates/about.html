About

Welcome to the Smart Contact Manager project! We're excited to introduce a modern and efficient solution for managing your contacts. Built on the powerful Spring Boot framework, our application provides an intuitive interface and advanced features to streamline your contact management experience.

At Smart Contact Manager, we understand the importance of staying connected with your network and maintaining strong relationships. Our goal is to simplify the process by offering a comprehensive set of tools and functionalities that will enhance your contact management workflow.

Key Features:

1. Contact Organization: Seamlessly store and organize all your contacts in one central location. Store image and email and phone

2. We can update contacts details like image, phone no, email address and work.

3. Advanced Search: Find the right contact quickly and effortlessly with our powerful search capabilities.

4. We can add multiple contacts.

5. User can login and use this application for store contacts.

6. User can update password login, logout with security

7. In this project implemented Security, Authentication on HTTP endpoints , Application Properties, Thymeleaf, Email

8. User can recover his account throught OTP
